ABOUT US

HOSPITALITY

Hospitality is the relationship between a guest and a host, wherein the host receives the guest with goodwill, including the reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers. Louis, chevalier de Jaucourt describes hospitality in the Encyclopédie as the virtue of a great soul that cares for the whole universe through the ties of humanity.

24/7 SERVICE

24-7 service is service that is available any time and, usually, every day. An alternate orthography for the numerical part includes 24×7 . The numerals stand for "24 hours a day, 7 days a week".

MANAGEMENT SKILLS
