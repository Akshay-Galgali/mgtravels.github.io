HONEYMOON PACKAGE

Destination: MUMBAI

Duration: 5 Days / 6 Nights

Day 01: As you arrive at Mumbai airport, you will be welcome by Travelogy India Representative. Transfer to hotel, Check in formalities, Overnight at Mumbai.

Day 02: Breakfast at hotel, Later Proceed to visit Gateway of India, Prince of wales, Dhobi Ghat, Victoria Terminus, Raja Bhai clock tower , Sidhivinayak Temple Transfer to hotel, Rest of the day relax, Overnight at Mumbai

Day 03: Breakfast at hotel then proceed for Dabbawala tour in first half and Bollywood tour in second half. Dabbawala begins at 10:00 and finished at 12:30 the word dabbawala implies a person in the Indian city of Mumbai who carries and delivers freshly made food from home in lunch boxes to office workers. Afternoon proceed for Bollywood Tour in which you get know about origin and evolution of Indian Film chance to watch shootings of Feature films, T.V serials or Ad Films, Apart from this it give you glimpse of the most famous Film Star Houses (from outside) located on Juhu and Bandra. Post tour drop back to hotel, Overnight at Mumbai.

Day 04: Breakfast at hotel then proceed for Elephanta caves excursions. Elephanta is island located 10km far from city. Day 05: Breakfast at hotel, Travelogy India representative would drop you at Mumbai Airport. Tour Terminates.
